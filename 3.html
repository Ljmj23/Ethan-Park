Unit & Union

Mix media

This installation artwork is about my place where I have all of my good childhood memories, which will soon disappear. In order to collect traces of nearby sites, alginates were used to replicate them through the soil, stones, and shape of roads. I also did some paintings that emphasize the feeling of the dirty construction site and made a simple sculpture from the construction site using wood and metal sticks. I tried to express these stones as they came out of the sculpture, I tried to express that I'm collecting some of my memories and recalling my memories.
