Why do you think I hate math?

Mix media

This work is created with stencil kit that allows me to secretly engrave a message I want to convey in a kind of personal secret code. I thought It would be interesting if I made a secret code based on mathematics, which is one of my least favorite things, and showed them what I wanted to talk about math. I used mathematical formulas, stereoscopic shapes, and graphs to replace each alphabet. Also, I thought that smudging or inaccuracies caused by stencil characteristics contrast with the exact concepts of mathematics. I tried to express my secret message on top of a strange-looking math note through the stencils of codes that replaced these alphabets.
